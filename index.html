About

The person behind the lens

I'm a travel photographer based between Cambridgeshire and wherever life takes me. I've been making photographs for seven years — first as a way to remember, then as a way to look more carefully.

My work focuses on ordinary moments in extraordinary places: the pause between two strangers, the geometry of a doorway, the colour of the sky at the exact wrong time.

Every photograph here has a story attached to it. Some are long. Some are just a feeling. I write them all down.
